Herman.

Your personal scribe.
Minimal, intuitive, endlessly customizable.

Create session notes with ease using inline variable insertion.

Just create a block, add a title, and then write your text including variables with a simple inline syntax. Just add in brackets and seperate the variables with a comma (i.e. [are, aren't]). Then when you add the block, just select the appropriate variable.

Built minimally, to maximize ease of use.

The app may look sparce, but hidden behind the minimalistic layout are powerful features. Every text block can have child blocks built beneath it, to allow you to fully create your normal flow-of-thought into the click-through interface.
